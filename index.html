Elon Musk

Biography

Elon Musk, born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, is a visionary entrepreneur and inventor known for his contributions to technology, transportation, and space exploration.

Musk co-founded PayPal and was instrumental in the creation of Tesla Inc., where he played a pivotal role in advancing electric vehicle technology. He also founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.

His ventures include SolarCity, Neuralink, and The Boring Company, each with the aim of pushing the boundaries of innovation and sustainability.

Innovative Spirit
